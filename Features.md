# DiscoveryOS MVP Features

## Core MVP Features

### 1. User Authentication & Account Management
- **Name**: User Authentication & Account Management
- **Purpose**: Enable secure user signup, login, and account management
- **Short Description**: Users can create accounts, log in securely, manage profile information, and reset passwords
- **Primary User**: All users (Product Managers, Founders, UX Researchers)

### 2. Workspace Creation & Management
- **Name**: Workspace Creation & Management
- **Purpose**: Provide isolated, collaborative environments for product teams
- **Short Description**: Users can create workspaces, invite team members, manage workspace settings, and control access levels
- **Primary User**: Workspace Admins, Product Leaders

### 3. Product Project Setup
- **Name**: Product Project Setup
- **Purpose**: Enable users to define a new product or feature for analysis
- **Short Description**: Users create a product project, specify product context, define target market, and set project objectives
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Founders

### 4. File Upload & Support
- **Name**: File Upload & Support
- **Purpose**: Allow users to upload customer research from multiple sources
- **Short Description**: Users can upload interview transcripts, survey responses, support tickets, meeting notes, and research documents in multiple formats (.txt, .docx, .pdf, .csv, .xlsx)
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, UX Researchers

### 5. Upload Status & Processing Queue
- **Name**: Upload Status & Processing Queue
- **Purpose**: Provide real-time visibility into file processing
- **Short Description**: Users see upload progress, processing status, estimated completion time, and queue position for multiple file uploads
- **Primary User**: All users

### 6. Customer Pain Point Extraction
- **Name**: Customer Pain Point Extraction
- **Purpose**: Automatically identify and extract recurring customer problems from research
- **Short Description**: AI agents analyze uploaded research to identify, extract, and catalog customer pain points with frequency counts and source attribution
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, UX Researchers

### 7. Theme & Pattern Detection
- **Name**: Theme & Pattern Detection
- **Purpose**: Group similar feedback into coherent themes and patterns
- **Short Description**: AI agents cluster related pain points, feature requests, and feedback into meaningful themes with descriptions and supporting evidence
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Researchers

### 8. Customer Segment Identification
- **Name**: Customer Segment Identification
- **Purpose**: Identify distinct customer groups affected by specific problems
- **Short Description**: AI agents analyze research to identify customer segments (by role, company size, industry, pain point) and map which segments have which problems
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Product Leaders

### 9. Insight Card View
- **Name**: Insight Card View
- **Purpose**: Display extracted insights in an easy-to-scan format
- **Short Description**: Users view pain points, themes, and customer segments as interactive cards with supporting quotes, frequency data, and source references
- **Primary User**: All users

### 10. Business Impact Assessment
- **Name**: Business Impact Assessment
- **Purpose**: Estimate the business value of addressing each identified problem
- **Short Description**: AI agents evaluate each pain point for market size, revenue opportunity, customer satisfaction impact, and urgency based on research data
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Product Leaders

### 11. Prioritization Dashboard
- **Name**: Prioritization Dashboard
- **Purpose**: Help teams decide what to build first
- **Short Description**: Visual dashboard displaying pain points/features ranked by business impact, customer segment size, frequency, and implementation complexity estimates
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Product Leaders

### 12. Product Discovery Report Generation
- **Name**: Product Discovery Report Generation
- **Purpose**: Create an executive-ready report summarizing all findings
- **Short Description**: Automatically generate a comprehensive PDF/HTML report including executive summary, key findings, customer segments, recommended priorities, and supporting evidence
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Product Leaders

### 13. Report Customization & Branding
- **Name**: Report Customization & Branding
- **Purpose**: Allow companies to customize reports with company branding
- **Short Description**: Users can customize report templates, add company logos, adjust color schemes, and include custom sections
- **Primary User**: Workspace Admins, Product Leaders

### 14. Report Export & Sharing
- **Name**: Report Export & Sharing
- **Purpose**: Enable easy distribution of findings to stakeholders
- **Short Description**: Users can export reports as PDF, share via shareable links, email reports to stakeholders, and control access permissions
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Product Leaders

### 15. Insight Search & Filter
- **Name**: Insight Search & Filter
- **Purpose**: Help users navigate and find specific insights quickly
- **Short Description**: Users can search insights by keyword, filter by theme, customer segment, pain point type, or source document
- **Primary User**: All users

### 16. Source Attribution & Traceability
- **Name**: Source Attribution & Traceability
- **Purpose**: Maintain audit trail and credibility of insights
- **Short Description**: Every insight includes links back to original source documents, quote references, and document metadata for verification and deep-dive research
- **Primary User**: All users

### 17. Team Collaboration on Insights
- **Name**: Team Collaboration on Insights
- **Purpose**: Enable teams to discuss and annotate findings
- **Short Description**: Team members can add comments, tags, and notes to insights, view discussion threads, and track discussion history
- **Primary User**: All workspace members

### 18. Manual Insight Creation & Editing
- **Name**: Manual Insight Creation & Editing
- **Purpose**: Allow users to add or modify insights based on team knowledge
- **Short Description**: Users can manually create pain points, themes, and segments; edit AI-generated insights; and add context or corrections
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Researchers

### 19. Insight Approval Workflow
- **Name**: Insight Approval Workflow
- **Purpose**: Establish quality control for insights before report generation
- **Short Description**: Team leads can review, approve, reject, or request changes to AI-generated insights before they appear in final reports
- **Primary User**: Product Managers, Team Leads

### 20. Basic Analytics & Metrics
- **Name**: Basic Analytics & Metrics
- **Purpose**: Track how research is being used and analyzed
- **Short Description**: Dashboard showing number of insights extracted, themes identified, customer segments found, and report generation count
- **Primary User**: Workspace Admins, Product Leaders

### 21. Project Activity Log
- **Name**: Project Activity Log
- **Purpose**: Maintain audit trail of project changes
- **Short Description**: View who uploaded files, when analysis completed, what insights were created/modified, and who accessed reports
- **Primary User**: Workspace Admins

### 22. Help & Documentation
- **Name**: Help & Documentation
- **Purpose**: Guide users through the platform
- **Short Description**: In-app help center, video tutorials, getting started guide, and contextual help for each feature
- **Primary User**: All users

### 23. Feedback & Support Channel
- **Name**: Feedback & Support Channel
- **Purpose**: Capture user feedback and support requests
- **Short Description**: Users can submit feedback, report issues, request features, and contact support directly from the platform
- **Primary User**: All users

### 24. Data Privacy & Security Settings
- **Name**: Data Privacy & Security Settings
- **Purpose**: Give users control over data handling
- **Short Description**: Users can configure data retention policies, manage encryption settings, control data export restrictions, and view privacy compliance information
- **Primary User**: Workspace Admins
